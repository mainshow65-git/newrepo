08 · FAQ

Answers, before you ask.

Everything you need to know before booking a demo.

Is ClinicFlow easy to use for non-technical staff?

Yes. The interface is built for front-desk teams — most are productive within a 45-minute session. We provide onboarding videos, live training and a dedicated support channel.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Absolutely. All plans include email support. Professional and Enterprise plans add priority chat & phone support, plus a dedicated account manager on Enterprise.

Is patient data secure and compliant?

Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We follow HIPAA-aligned security practices, role-based access, and maintain audit-ready logs for every action in the system.

Can it integrate with my existing systems?

Yes. We import from Excel, Google Sheets and most legacy EMRs. Professional and Enterprise plans include full API access for custom integrations.

Is there a free trial?

Every plan includes a 14-day free trial — no credit card required. You also get a guided onboarding call with our team to set you up for success.
